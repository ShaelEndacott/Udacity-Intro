Grey Wolf

Many species of wolf are now extinct, and others are on the World Conservation Union's Red List of Threatened Species.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Weight and Height: 30 in (76 cm), 100 lbs (45 kg)
  • Average Lifespan: 6-8 years
  • Habitat: North America, Eurasia, and Africa

The grey wolf is a large canid. It is a predator that is at or near the top of the food chain. As its range has expanded, it has once again come into contact with humans. The effects of wolf-human interaction in North America have led to the deaths of many wolves and impeded its return to its former habitat.
